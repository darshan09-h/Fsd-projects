Featured Hotels

The Lalit Grand Palace

Designed by Maharaja Pratap Singh in 1910, The LaLiT Grand Palace stands as a witness to India's history. Surrounded by the Himalayas and overlooking Dal Lake, it offers 113 palace guest rooms, 10 cottages, banqueting space, conference facilities, and a shopping arcade.

The St. Regis Goa Resort

The St. Regis Goa Resort offers a luxury experience in South Goa, India, set amidst 49 acres of lush greenery. Find us within driving distance of wildlife sanctuaries, waterfalls and spice plantations.

Featured Activities

The Lalit Grand Palace

Shikara Ride on Dal Lake: Experience the thrill of exploring the serene waters of Dal Lake aboard a traditional Kashmiri Shikara arranged by The LaLiT Grand Palace. Glide across the tranquil lake, surrounded by snow-capped mountains, lush gardens, and floating markets, as you immerse yourself in the natural beauty and cultural charm of Srinagar.

The St. Regis Goa Resort

Scuba Diving Expedition: Dive into the azure waters of the Arabian Sea and explore Goa's vibrant underwater world with a scuba diving expedition organized by The St. Regis, Goa. Discover colorful coral reefs, tropical fish, and marine life as you descend into the depths of the ocean, experiencing the thrill of adventure and the wonders of the aquatic realm firsthand.

Rambagh Palace

Hot Air Balloon Safari: Experience the thrill of soaring high above the picturesque landscapes of Jaipur with a hot air balloon safari arranged by Rambagh Palace. Drift gracefully over the vibrant cityscape, majestic forts, and lush countryside as you enjoy panoramic views of Rajasthan's iconic landmarks and natural beauty from a unique perspective.
